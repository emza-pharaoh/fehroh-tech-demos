Who We Are

A legacy of strategic economic thinking

Shengele & Company is a boutique advisory firm with deep expertise in local and regional economic development across Southern Africa. Our multidisciplinary team combines rigorous analytical capability with decades of on-the-ground implementation experience.

We serve as trusted advisors to governments, development finance institutions, and forward-thinking private sector clients who seek to build inclusive, resilient economies from the ground up.
